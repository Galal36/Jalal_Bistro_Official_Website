Our Story

Serving authentic flavors with passion since 2010

Our Vision

At Galal's Bistro, we don't just serve food - we create experiences. Founded by Galal Ali, our restaurant has been a pioneer in bringing authentic Egyptian cuisine with a modern twist to Assiut. We believe in creativity, quality, and the power of a shared meal to bring people together.

Kosher Certified

We take pride in our Kosher certification, ensuring that all our dishes meet the highest standards of quality and preparation. This certification reflects our commitment to excellence in every aspect of our service.

Our Philosophy

Every dish at Galal's Bistro tells a story - of tradition, of innovation, and of passion. We source the freshest local ingredients and combine them with time-honored recipes to create flavors that delight both the palate and the soul.

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team of chefs and staff are the heart of our restaurant. With years of experience and a genuine love for hospitality, they ensure every visit to Galal's Bistro is memorable.
